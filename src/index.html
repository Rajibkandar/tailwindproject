About us

About us

The word Rehmat means 'Blessings', 'Grace', and 'Compassion'.

The Rehmat Foundation is dedicated to empowering vulnerable individuals and communities through holistic development, self-sufficiency, and resilience.

Our mission is deeply rooted in compassion and empowerment. We strive to address pressing societal issues through four key pillars: food donation initiatives, rehabilitation programs for former justice-involved individuals, educational support for students who are in need, and combat social isolation among the elderly

Through our efforts, we strive to build resilient communities where every person is valued and empowered to reach their full potential.
