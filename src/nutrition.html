Food Donation Program

Food Donation to Vulnerable Communities

Bringing Joy with Every Meal

At TRF, we believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right. Through our food donation programs, we aim to alleviate hunger and food insecurity among vulnerable populations in Ontario. By partnering with local food banks, shelters, and community organizations, we distribute essential food items to those in need.

In this program, we aim to provide food to the homeless every Sunday, consisting of fruits, a nutritious meal, and juice, to address food insecurity and promote well-being among those living on the streets.

With every honest effort, we ensure that the meals we provide are not only delicious but also prepared with utmost cleanliness and hygiene.
